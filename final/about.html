My journey in design has been fueled by a deep fascination with understanding human interaction and behavior. I believe that the key to exceptional design lies in empathizing with users, understanding their needs, and aligning design solutions with those needs. I am driven by the idea of crafting digital spaces that resonate with users, making their interactions seamless, enjoyable, and meaningful.

Beyond the world of design, I find joy in exploring my limitless creativity through color pencil drawings and watercolor painting. Plein-air scenes are my favorite subjects, but most notably, capturing things I appreciate and admire, which push the boundaries of human interaction.

In my design journey, I want to craft visually appealing interfaces that evoke positive emotions in users. Attention to detail is key, and I am constantly experimenting and iterating in these areas:

User Research
